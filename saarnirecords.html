Saarni Records

Amazing music releasing since 2016

Saarni Records was founded by Markus Heinonen in 2016. A musician since 1999 and an avid recording engineer since 2004. Being a music lover and producing enthusiast all his life, Saarni Music was eventually founded when Markus found that no studio would offer a solution that utilises modern, "quality of life" technological improvements in music production, while cherishing the human aspects and polishing the dirt out of the way so the soul of the music could shine, instead of polishing the soul out of the music.
Saarni Records eventually exploded into multiple facets covering the music production and business in general. Throughout the years it has become clear that most record studios, record labels, event organisers and live clubs exist solely for the purpose to exploit artists in greedy pursuits. Saarni Music was found and is today more relevant than ever in bringing amazing art to life and working on making the artists flourish.
