INFO

Saarni Music was founded by Markus Heinonen, a musician since 1999 and a passionate recording engineer since 2004. Markus has always been dedicated to the art of music production and the joy it brings to people's lives. His mission was to create a studio that could harness the latest technological advancements in the industry to make the production process more efficient, while still maintaining a human touch and a commitment to quality.
At the beginning of 2016 Markus had aquired the education, insight, location and the community to start the journey as an entrepreneur. He had worked with numerous recording studios, live clubs, record labels and realized that the resources available to aspiring musicians were mostly business oriented, where as the optimal studio and creative space should be more focused on creative endeavours and the demand for such a resource was real.
With this vision in mind, Saarni Music was born in 2016. Since then, the studio has expanded to offer a wide range of services, including recording, producing, mixing, mastering, and even rental space for live shows, music videos, and photoshoots. Our team is dedicated to bringing out the best in every artist and helping them achieve their musical goals and dreams.
We understand that recording and producing music can be a complex and daunting process. That's why we offer expert consultation and teaching services to help guide you through every step of the way. Whether you're a seasoned musician or a complete beginner, we're here to help you realize your musical potential!

Recording
Recording is the process of capturing audio material and saving it as tracks into the recording software.

"Recording consists of multiple variables that affect the final outcome (time available, gear used, genre connotations that apply, energy levels of everyone involved, end result we aim for, etc). It's about capturing the essence of the music, the emotion, and the vibe of the moment. We have the understanding and a well equipped facility for the most pristine sound quality possible. We will work with you every step of the way to ensure that your recording is nothing short of spectacular"

Music Production
Music production is about making the song shine. Adding the right elements at the right time, bringing out the best in the artist(s) and the music.

"The process of music production involves every detail that goes into making a record (outside of the technical aspects of recording and mixing), working towards the end result. Producing can begin before recording or even before songwriting, going all the way to the mastering phase. It can involve, but not limited to: Writing music or lyrics, arrangement, personal guidance, sound design, etc..."

Mixing
Mixing is where everything comes together. All the recorded elements become a part of one cohesive whole entity of sound.

"Mixing is the part that makes or breaks the record. In order to become classics, great performances and amazing songs require a mix that not only sound great but feel great as well. Saarni Studio mix engineer Markus has done hundreds of mixes since 2004 and aims to evolve every time. Besides working with the pristine recordings of Saarni Studio, we are also aware of the trend of independent artists recording with low budgets or doing it all by themselves, and we are perfectly capable of dealing with those recordings and mix them into a presentable form."

Mastering
Mastering is the final step in the music production process. It's where the mixed song gets the final polish.

"Saarni Music has years of experience and a wealth of knowledge when it comes to achieving the perfect master. We will ensure that your music sounds amazing on every platform, from streaming services to vinyl records."

Rental Space
Saarni Studio is not just a recording studio, it's a creative space

"Our studio can be rented out for various activities, from live shows to music videos, photoshoots, and more. Our space is designed to inspire creativity and provide artists with everything they need to bring their ideas to life."

Teaching & Consultation
Private lessons for aspiring audio engineers and consultation for groups in their creative endeavours.

"At Saarni Music, we believe that music is a journey, and we are dedicated to helping artists at every step. Our founder has dedicated his life to music and has learned many lessons along the way. He is passionate about sharing his knowledge and helping others achieve their dreams."
